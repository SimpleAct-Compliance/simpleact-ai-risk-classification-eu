# Common Classification Errors

## "If the vendor says low risk, we are done"

Provider language is not a substitute for internal assessment.

## "Everything important is high-risk"

High impact and high-risk are not always the same category.

## "If it is not high-risk, it needs no governance"

Internal governance risk can still be substantial.

## "Classification is a one-time task"

Changes in use case, provider, deployment, or audience can require reassessment.

## "Business risk and legal category are identical"

These should be evaluated separately even when they influence each other.
